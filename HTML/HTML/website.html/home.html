Huracan STO

Huracan STO s all set to launch the Huracan STO in the country on 15 July, 2021. The Italian automobile manufacturer, which unveiled the model in November 2020, will introduce the race-car-inspired V10 version in India later this month. Taking inspiration from models such as the Huracan GT3 and the Huracan Super Trofeo Evo, the new Lamborghini Huracan STO gets a revised exterior design. Up-front, the model features a single element that combines various body parts such as the bumper, fenders, and hood. The rear profile features a tweaked bumper design, the signature dual-exhaust setup, and a massive adjustable rear wing. Also on offer are three new drive modes (STO, Rain, and Trophy), a roof scoop, and Brembo CCM-R carbon-ceramic brakes.
